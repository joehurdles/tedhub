Who we are

TED Hub was established to promote entrepreneurial spirit among the youth with training programs, outreaches and support SME’s with business advisory services and funding opportunities within the entrepreneurial ecosystem in Africa.

Since our inception our core mandate has been on introducing the youth within the municipality to alternate sources of developing sustainable economic livelihood through entrepreneurial action. As concerned members of the community, we decided to do something about the growing number of youths going into galamsey or seeing employment in the few mining companies within the community as the bottom line for success. We work hand in hand with the municipal assembly and traditional council. We also provide entrepreneurial and technological trainings to students within University of Mines and Technology (UMaT), Tarkwa Senior High School and Fiaseman Senior High School. We are active members of AfriLabs which is the network of all technology and business incubators across Africa. TED Hub is also a recognized member of Ghana Hubs Network.

TED Hub is the initiative of two Computer Science and Engineering graduates from the University of Mines and Technology. We both grew in different mining communities in Ghana but had no desire of becoming miners. We wanted to be our own bosses by helping others find their way too. The goal is leverage on creativity,innovation and our skills to solve societal challenges whiles building sustainable and scalable businesses. We committed ourselves to learning and getting acquainted with the ecosystem leaving a trail of positive impact along the way. We’re not there yet but we will get there.

Our Services

What We Do

Our community of business and technology experts, support us in the provision of quality entrepreneurship and technical trainings throughout the Tarkwa Municipality.

TRAINING PROGRAMS

We work with a team of Business Experts from the Faculty of Integrated Management Sciences and Office of Research, Innovation and Consultancy of UMaT to provide business advisory services that helps entrepreneurs sustain and scale their businesses.

BUSINESS CONSULTING

We organize excursions to various mining sites. This provides our entrepreneurs the rare experience of life within these communities to aid them innovate and come out with feasible business solutions to make our society better.

MINE TOURS

We provide co-working space for entrepreneurs within our community. We use this as a soft cushion of young startups to get a base of operation and also to tap into expertise within the community.
