TED Hub was established to promote entrepreneurial spirit among the youth with training programs, outreaches and support SME’s with business advisory services and funding opportunities within the entrepreneurial ecosystem in Africa. Since our inception our core mandate has been on introducing the youth within the municipality to alternate sources of developing sustainable economic livelihood through entrepreneurial action. As concerned members of the community, we decided to do something about the growing number of youths going into galamsey or seeing employment in the few mining companies within the community as the bottom line for success.

TED Hub was established to promote entrepreneurial spirit among the youth with training programs, outreaches and support SME’s with business advisory services and funding opportunities within the entrepreneurial ecosystem in Africa. Since our inception our core mandate has been on introducing the youth within the municipality to alternate sources of developing sustainable economic livelihood through entrepreneurial action. As concerned members of the community, we decided to do something about the growing number of youths going into galamsey or seeing employment in the few mining companies within the community as the bottom line for success. We work hand in hand with the municipal assembly and traditional council. We also provide entrepreneurial and technological trainings to students within University of Mines and Technology (UMaT), Tarkwa Senior High School and Fiaseman Senior High School. We are active members of AfriLabs which is the network of all technology and business incubators across Africa. TED Hub is also a recognized member of Ghana Hubs Network.

Simon Asare

Co Founder
